About the developer

Daniel Stutzman is a software developer living in Boulder, CO. He works as a consultant, particularly in back-end web development with Ruby on Rails.

Daniel gained familiarity with artificial intelligence by working on the speech recognition team at Rosetta Stone, a company that makes language learning software. He also studied natural language processing at the University of Colorado and the Linguistic Institute.

His passion for music and sight reading began with piano lessons as a child. He hopes that Song Reader will help beginning musicians to gain comfort with music notation and ultimately to improve their music literacy.
