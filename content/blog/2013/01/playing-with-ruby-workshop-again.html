On Saturday January 5 I'm leading a workshop titled "Playing with Ruby" at DaVinci Institute in Louisville, CO -- the same place where I'll be teaching Ruby on Rails starting January 14. I've led this same workshop before.

The central idea is: instead of giving participants a bunch of parts and instructions about how to put them together, we'll give them some some working software and they can learn by tweaking.
