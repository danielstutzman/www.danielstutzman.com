My coworker Jessica Dillon and I are leading one of the workshops the day before Rocky Mountain Ruby conference in Boulder, CO. It's titled Playing With Ruby, it's at 9 a.m. on Tuesday, September 18, and you can read more about it on the list of workshops.

The central idea is: instead of giving participants a bunch of parts and instructions about how to put them together, we'll give them some some working software and they can learn by tweaking.
