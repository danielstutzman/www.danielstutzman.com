--- layout: post title: Why blog? date: 2011-02-20 ---

This personal blog exists for the following reasons:

  • To reveal what kind of person I am, as writing tends to do
  • To provide technical answers discoverable by searching
  • To give me practice at writing publicly
  • To recommend books and online writing I enjoy to others
  • To boost my search rankings by providing continual content updates

Don't expect much in the way of:

  • syndicated advertising
  • photos and videos
  • comments
  • editorials on world news
  • embarrassing anecdotes
