--- layout: post title: Prevent iPhone's automatic hyperlinking of numbers date: 2011-03-04 ---

If you browse to your web site with Safari on iOS, you might notice that numbers with dashes (for example, 2003-2004) can incorrectly become hyperlinks (for example, 2003-2004). Â According to the relevant page in Apple's documentation, you can disable this behavior by adding the following meta tag:

<meta name = "format-detection" content = "telephone=no">
